# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Architecture-firm owners, architects, site engineers, operations staff, and clients use Vastos Arch to coordinate projects, estimates, procurement, payments, documentation, and site progress. Internal users often work quickly across dense operational data; clients need a calmer, more guided view of the same project truth.

## Product Purpose

Vastos Arch gives an architecture practice one dependable operating surface from lead through handover. Success means users can understand project health, find exceptions, make routine updates, and communicate status without hunting through disconnected tools.

## Brand Personality

Precise, composed, and quietly confident. The product should feel established and dependable, with the taste level of a design-led professional practice rather than the visual mannerisms of a generic startup template.

## Anti-references

Avoid cookie-cutter SaaS dashboards, generic bordered card grids, nested containers, purple-blue startup gradients, decorative glassmorphism, excessive pills, weak gray-on-gray hierarchy, inconsistent spacing, and visual novelty that competes with the work. Do not imitate any reference product literally.

## Design Principles

- Make the next important decision obvious through hierarchy, not decoration.
- Treat operational density as a craft problem: compact where comparison matters, spacious where comprehension matters.
- Let architecture-practice confidence show through exact alignment, measured rhythm, and restrained color.
- Keep client-facing surfaces reassuring and plainspoken while preserving the same system vocabulary.
- Make state and feedback unmistakable without making the interface loud.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for contrast, focus visibility, keyboard access, reduced motion, and semantic structure. Never rely on color alone to communicate status. Keep controls comfortably targetable and data legible at browser zoom up to 200%.
